"There is no limit to what we, as women, can accomplish."

Michelle Obama

Many of life’s most practical skills and interesting industries have, for some reason, been historically deemed “men’s things”. Car troubles? Ask a man. Financial questions? Ask a man. Sports? You know what I’m talking about. Because of this, many women believe it is too late to start learning these things, or doubt their ability to do so. You Pro, Girl! is a community of supportive women who know that women can do and be anything they choose, especially experts in “men’s things”.
